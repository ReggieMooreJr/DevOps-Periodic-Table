DevOps - Let's CrossPollinate

It is the practice of operations and development engineers participating together in the entire service lifecycle, from design through the development process to production support.
Dev is used as shorthand for developers in particular, but really in practice it is even wider and means “all the people involved in developing the product,” which can include Product, QA, and other kinds of disciplines.
